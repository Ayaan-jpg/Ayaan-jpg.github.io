    History

    Christmas celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ, on the 25th of December, in 0AD. The calendar begins at his birth. Santa Clause is based off someone called St.Nicholas, who, in the late 13th and early 14th centuries, would generously and anonymously go around the neighbourhood at night, giving children small gifts, like toys and food, and leaving sacks of coins in the rooms of the needy. Nowadays, on Christmas Eve night, it is said that Santa Clause, also known as Father Christmas, St Nick, and Kris Kringle, rides his flying 8-reindeer-sleigh across the world, led by Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer, leaving the desired gifts under the Christmas trees or stockings of children. Throughout the year, in his headquarters in the North Pole, whilst the elves work hard making the gifts for children, Santa watches every boy and girl, and choose whether they should be on the naughty or nice list. The children on the nice list get what they ask for on Christmas, but the naughty ones find coal in their stockings!
